DON’T: DRY IT!

Never ever EVER throw your bra in the dryer, it destroys it. Now, they recommend hand washing your bra as well….but I, of course, have no patience for that, so I came up with a cheat. Tide came up with a bra bag – basically a lingerie bag except circular and can fit 2 bras (a zipper to a little compartment on either side). So far using this prevents distortion/damage and also prevents a tangled mess in the washer.

DON’T: Think that all push-ups are the same

“Just because two bras are push-ups does not mean that they will push up the same way, for example the gorgeous and fabulous bras at Victoria’s Secret. Yes, both push up, but both have different types of padding. The gorgeous is more of a marshmallow feeling padding where it is more comfortable to wear – it feels like it is not there; while the fabulous has actual cream in the padding, this helps get a more natural looking push-up and helps hide any imperfections the women may feel she has.”

DON’T: Think that all brand’s sizes run the same

“Store sizes are a little different. VS bras tend to be a little tighter, so don’t get frightened if you’re measured at something that you’re normally not use to. And don’t be afraid to ask for help. The consultants at VS have to get certified on how to fit people and every store has a bra expert lead. She is the one with the most experience and the most knowledge of all the bras the store carries. If you don’t agree with a fitting from one of the other consultants I would recommend talking to her, she normally is wearing a name badge that says bra expert.”
